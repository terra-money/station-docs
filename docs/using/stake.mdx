# Stake

You can stake your assets in Station to earn staking rewards. Follow the steps below to learn how to stake. For more information on staking, visit the [Terra Docs](https://docs.terra.money/learn/protocol#staking). 

1. Visit [https://dashboard.station.money/](https://dashboard.station.money/) in your browser and connect your Station wallet extension. 

2. In the menu on the left, click **Stake**. 

3. On the Staking page, you'll see a list of assets. The stakeable assets that you own will have a blue **Stake** button next to them. To stake an asset, select the asset by clicking the **Stake** button next to the asset you want to stake. 

<img
    className="readme-img-lg"
    src="/img/screens/stake/stake-1.png"
    alt="stake" 
    />


<Admonition type="caution" icon="ðŸ’°" title="undelegating periods">

When you stake your assets, you are locking them up in exchange for rewards. Staked assets cannot be traded freely. While you still own the assets you stake, you will not be able to trade them freely until you udelegate them and wait for the undelegating period to end. Terra's undelegating period is 21 days, meaning you will have to wait 21 days to receive your Luna after undelegating it. The unstaking period varies depending on the chain the asset is staked to. 

You can [redelegate](#redelegate) a staked asset to another validator without having to wait for the unstaking period. 

For more information on staking, visit the [Terra Docs](https://docs.terra.money/learn/protocol#staking). 

</Admonition>


## Auto stake

Not sure which validator you'd like to stake with? Auto-staking solves that problem by randomly selecting a validator that charges less than 5% commission and is not in the top 30% by voting power in order to improve decentralization. To auto-stake you can follow the steps below

1. Click **Auto-stake**. 


<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/stake/stake-2.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

2. Enter the amount you would like to stake. Remember, once an asset is staked, you will need to wait until the end of the unstaking period before you can claim it. Click **Confirm**. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/stake/stake-3.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

3. Review the transaction in your Station extension and click **Post**. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/stake/stake-4.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

## Claim rewards

After staking your assets to a validator, they will start to accrue staking rewards. You can claim your rewards periodically by following the steps below.

1. Visit [https://dashboard.station.money/](https://dashboard.station.money/) in your browser and connect your Station wallet extension. 

2. In the menu on the left, click **Stake**. 

3. If you have staked assets that are earning rewards, you will see a button in the top left corner that says **Claim all rewards**. Click the button.

## Undelegate

<Admonition type="caution" icon="ðŸ’°" title="Undelegating periods">

When you stake your assets, you are locking them up in exchange for rewards. Staked assets cannot be traded freely. While you still own the assets you stake, you will not be able to trade them freely until you undelegate them and wait for the undelegating period to end. Terra's undelegating period is 21 days, meaning you will have to wait 21 days to receive your Luna after undelegating it. The unstaking period varies depending on the chain the asset is staked to. 

You can [redelegate](#redelegate) a staked asset to another validator without having to wait for the unstaking period. 

For more information on staking, visit the [Terra Docs](https://docs.terra.money/learn/protocol#staking). 

</Admonition>

1. Follow the steps above [to manage your staked assets](#manage-your-staked-assets). Click the **Manage** button next to the validator you want to undelegate from. 

<img
    className="readme-img-lg"
    src="/img/screens/stake/manage-1.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

2. On the validator delegation page, click **Undelegate**. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/stake/manage-2.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

3. Enter the amount you want to undelegate and click **Confirm**. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/stake/manage-3.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

4. Review and sign the transaction in your Station extension and click **Post**. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/stake/manage-4.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

5. Your assets are undelegating. After waiting for the undelegating period to end, return to your staking page in Station to claim your undelegated assets. 

## Redelegate

If you want to move your staked assets from one delegator to another, you can do so without having to wait for the stake to undelegate. Follow the steps below to redelegate assets to another validator. 

1. Follow the steps above [to manage your staked assets](#manage-your-staked-assets). Click the **Manage** button next to the validator you want to undelegate from. 

<img
    className="readme-img-lg"
    src="/img/screens/stake/manage-1.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

2. On the validator delegation page, click **Redelegate**. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/stake/manage-2.png"
    alt="stake"
    />

3. You can either choose to Auto stake or manually stake the amount you want to redelegate. Follow the [Auto stake](#auto-stake) or [manual stake](#manual-stake) instructions to complete your transaction.
